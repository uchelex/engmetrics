    About This Website

    This website is a collection of practical, easy-to-use calculators designed to support students, engineers, researchers, and professionals.

    The primary goal is to provide fast, transparent, and accessible calculation tools for engineering, science, and general technical applications—without unnecessary complexity.

    What This Site Does

    This site offers calculators and converters covering areas such as:

    • Engineering and scientific calculations
    • Unit conversions (SI, engineering, and field units)
    • Statistics and data analysis tools
    • Energy, fuel, and emissions estimations
    • Petroleum, drilling, and production-related calculations
    • General-purpose technical utilities

    All tools are designed to run directly in the browser, requiring no downloads or registrations.

    Who This Site Is For

    This site is built for:

    • Engineering and science students
    • Practicing engineers and technical professionals
    • Researchers and educators
    • Anyone who needs quick, reliable calculations

    The calculators are intended for educational and informational purposes only and should not replace professional judgment or certified engineering software.

    Accuracy and Transparency

    Every effort is made to ensure that formulas and methods used in the calculators follow standard engineering and scientific conventions. However, results may depend on assumptions, input quality, and rounding.

    Users are encouraged to verify critical results independently, especially when used in real-world applications.

    How the Site Is Supported

    This website is supported through advertising and may display ads from third-party services such as Google AdSense. Advertising helps keep the calculators free and accessible to everyone.

    Ads are clearly separated from calculation results and do not influence the outputs of any calculator.

    Privacy and Cookies

    The site uses cookies and similar technologies to:

    • Serve and measure advertisements
    • Understand how visitors use the site
    • Improve performance and usability

    No calculators require personal identification details to operate.

    Ongoing Development

    The site is continuously improved with new calculators, better visualizations, and clearer explanations. Feedback and suggestions are welcome and help guide future updates.

    Contact

    For questions, feedback, or suggestions, please use the contact information provided on the site.

    This website is provided as-is, without warranties of any kind, and is intended for general informational use.
