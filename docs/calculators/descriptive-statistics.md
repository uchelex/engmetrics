# Descriptive Statistics Calculator

This calculator computes the **mean**, **variance**, and **standard deviation** of a dataset.

**Input format:**
- Enter numbers separated by commas
- Spaces are allowed and ignored

**Example:**  
`2, 4, 6, 8, 10`
