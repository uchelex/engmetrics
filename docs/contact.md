# Contact

Thank you for your interest in this website.

This site is designed to provide free, browser-based calculators and technical tools for educational and informational purposes. Feedback from users helps improve accuracy, usability, and future calculator development.

---

## How to Get in Touch

You may reach us directly via email:

**Email:** [Click here to contact us](mailto:ifeanyi.oguamah@futo.edu.ng)

You may contact us for:

* Feedback on existing calculators
* Suggestions for new calculators or features
* Reporting errors or unexpected behavior
* General questions about how the site works

Please note that **individual technical consulting or professional engineering advice is not provided** through this site.

---

## What to Include in Your Message

To help us respond effectively, please include:

* The name of the calculator or page you are referring to
* A brief description of the issue or suggestion
* Example input values (if reporting a calculation issue)

Avoid including sensitive or personal information in your message.

---

## Response Time

Messages are reviewed periodically. While we aim to read all feedback, responses may not be immediate.

---

## Advertising and Partnerships

For advertising-related inquiries, partnerships, or general site-related questions, please clearly indicate this in your message subject.

---

## Privacy Notice

This site does not require user accounts or personal data to operate calculators. Any information you voluntarily provide in a message is used solely for communication purposes and is not shared with third parties.
